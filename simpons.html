The Simpsons

The most dysfunctional family on TV.

Homer J. Simpson

The safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer is known for his laziness, love of beer and donuts, and his iconic catchphrase: "D'oh!" Despite his flaws, he loves his family deeply, often in his own destructive way.

D'oh! Donuts Nuclear Safety

Marge Simpson

The long-suffering, yet incredibly patient, matriarch of the family. Marge is the moral center, constantly trying to maintain order amid the chaos created by her husband and son. Her famous blue beehive is instantly recognizable.

Blue Hair Moral Compass Patience

Bart Simpson

The mischievous ten-year-old troublemaker. Bart is known for his pranks, skateboard, and catchphrases like "Ay Caramba!" and "Eat my shorts." He constantly butts heads with Principal Skinner and his sister, Lisa.

Ay Caramba! Skateboarding Pranks

Lisa Simpson

The intelligent and compassionate eight-year-old prodigy. Lisa is a vegetarian, a Buddhist, and an avid jazz saxophone player. She often serves as the family's conscience, though she frequently feels isolated by her intellect.

Saxophone Genius Activist

Maggie Simpson

The perpetually silent baby of the family. Maggie communicates mostly through pacifier sucking and occasionally demonstrating incredible feats of intelligence or heroism. She is fiercely protective of her family.

Pacifier Silent Heroic
